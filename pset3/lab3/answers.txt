sort1 uses: Bubble Sort

How do you know?: It sorts fastest on already sorted arrays due do it having a big Omega of n with a lower bound on order of n steps.

sort2 uses: Merge Sort

How do you know?: It sorts fasted on random unordered arrays due to it having a big O of nlogn with an upper boung on order of nlogn steps.

sort3 uses: Selection sort

How do you know?: It sorts slowest on already sorted arrays of numbers due to it having a big Omega of n^2 with a lower bound on order of n^2 steps.
